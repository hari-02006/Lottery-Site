This is a promotional lottery.

No guaranteed winnings.

Winning is based on chance.

Ads support site operation and are not mandatory for participation.

Participation

  1. Users can generate one lottery code every 2 minutes per browser/device.
  2. Each valid submitted code counts as one lottery entry.
  3. Codes are random and intended for single use.

Submission Method

  1. Users submit codes using the linked Google Form.
  2. Required fields: Lottery Code, Nickname/Username, Human Check.
  3. Optional field: Email for winner contact only.

Validation and Winner Selection

  1. Entries are reviewed and duplicate or suspicious submissions may be removed.
  2. Winner is selected randomly from valid entries.
  3. Results are announced on this website.

Ad Policy Notes

  1. No auto-loading click ads.
  2. No forced ad interaction.
  3. No auto-refresh behavior to increase ad views.
